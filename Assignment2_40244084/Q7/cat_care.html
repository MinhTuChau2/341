Cat Care


  • Provide safe and clean environemnt
  • Set up a litter box and teach your cat to use it
  • Keep fresh water available for the dog at all times
  • Get the cat mentally stimulating toys
  • Buy the most appropriate food for your cat based on his weight and age. Ask a professional if not sure
  • Groom the cat regularly even if they self clean. It is important to brush them
  • Train your cat to use a scratcher for their nails
  • Don't forget giving lots of love to your cat!
