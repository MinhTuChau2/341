Dog Care


  • Provide a safe and clean environment
  • Keep fresh water available for the dog at all times
  • Allocate play time and walk time for your dog everyday
  • Train your dog
  • Groom him every three months
  • Buy the most appropriate food for your dog based on his weight and age. Ask a professional if not sure
  • Take him to the vet regularly for check ups
  • Don't forget giving lots of love to your dog!
